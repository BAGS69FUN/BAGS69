BAGS69 is a fair launch presale platform built on BAGS.FM. It allows anyone to create token presales where the creator and up to 68 participants contribute SOL and automatically receive trading fee shares forever.

Unlike traditional presales, BAGS69 uses a weighted fee-sharing model - all participants earn trading fees proportional to their SOL contribution, creating aligned incentives between creators and supporters.

üíé Fee Structure

BAGS69 uses a transparent weighted fee-sharing model that rewards contributors proportionally to their SOL investment:

Creator Fee Share (Deployer) 5% of trading fees
Participants Fee Share 95% (weighted by SOL contribution)
Max Participants 68 wallets
Launch Fee (Creator) 0.045 SOL (non-refundable)
Min Contribution 0.01 SOL
Max Contribution 0.1 SOL
Weighted Distribution: Your share of the 95% participant pool is based on how much SOL you contributed relative to the total. Contribute more = earn more fees!

üìù How to Create a Presale

Creating a presale on BAGS69 is simple:

1

Connect Wallet & Pay Fee

Connect your Solana wallet and pay the 0.045 SOL launch fee (non-refundable). This makes you the deployer with 5% fee share.

2

Configure Token

Enter token name, symbol, description, and upload an image. Add Twitter/website if you have them.

3

Set Participant Count

Choose between 1-68 participant slots. Each participant can contribute 0.01-0.1 SOL.

4

Wait for Participants

Share your presale link. Once all slots fill, the token auto-launches on BAGS.FM!

5

Claim Fees Forever

Visit the Claim Fees page anytime to collect your accumulated trading fee rewards.

‚ùì FAQ

What happens if the presale doesn't fill?
All participant contributions are fully refunded. Only the creator's 0.045 SOL launch fee is non-refundable.
How are fees calculated?
Creator gets 5% of all trading fees. The remaining 95% is distributed to participants based on their SOL contribution weight.
Can I contribute multiple times?
Each wallet can only join once per presale, contributing between 0.01-0.1 SOL.
When can I claim fees?
Anytime after the token launches! Fees accumulate from trading activity and can be claimed whenever you want.
